Smart Watering

  • Water early in the morning or late at night to reduce evaporation.
  • Use a watering can instead of a hose to target plant roots directly.
  • Install a soil moisture sensor to avoid overwatering.
  • Group plants with similar water needs together.
üåµ

Choose Native Plants

  • Select plants that are native to your region.
  • Native plants are better adapted to the local climate.
  • They require less water and are more resistant to pests.
  • Support local biodiversity by choosing native species.
üçÇ

Use Mulch

  • Apply a layer of mulch around your plants.
  • Mulch helps soil retain moisture by slowing evaporation.
  • It also suppresses weeds that compete for water.
  • Use organic mulch like wood chips or straw.
