Understanding the Water Crisis

Our planet's most vital resource is under threat. Learn about the two primary challenges we face.

Water Scarcity

The lack of sufficient available water resources to meet the demands of a region.

  • Physical Scarcity: Not enough water in nature to meet demand, common in arid regions.
  • Economic Scarcity: Water is available, but infrastructure (pipes, wells) is lacking to deliver it.

Water Pollution

The contamination of water bodies, making it unsafe for human use and disrupting ecosystems.

  • Point Source: Pollution from a single, identifiable source like a factory drainpipe.
  • Non-point Source: Widespread pollution from diffuse sources, like agricultural runoff.
